About Shree Anna Platform

Connecting the millet value chain for sustainable agriculture and nutrition security

Our Mission

To create a comprehensive digital marketplace that connects farmers, FPOs, SHGs, transporters, dealers, startups, processors, and consumers for sustainable millet trading and awareness.

We believe in promoting the nutritional benefits of millets (Shree Anna) while supporting the livelihoods of farmers and creating a transparent, traceable supply chain.

Our Vision

Global Impact

To become the leading platform for millet trading, promoting sustainable agriculture globally

Community Building

Creating a strong community of farmers, processors, and consumers working together

Sustainability

Promoting climate-resilient crops and sustainable farming practices

Nutrition Security

Ensuring access to nutritious millet-based products for all
