Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a farmer?

Click on the "Register" button and select "Farmer" as your role. Fill in your details and start listing your millet crops on the platform.

How does the traceability system work?

Our platform tracks millet products from farm to consumer, providing complete transparency about origin, processing, and transportation.

What types of millets are supported?

We support all major millet types including Finger Millet (Ragi), Pearl Millet (Bajra), Foxtail Millet, Little Millet, Kodo Millet, Proso Millet, and Barnyard Millet.

How do I become a transporter?

Register as a "Transporter" and you'll be able to view and accept transport requests from farmers and dealers.

Is there a mobile app available?

Currently, we have a responsive web application that works well on mobile devices. A dedicated mobile app is in development.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us through the contact form above, email us at support@shreeannaplatform.com, or call us at +91 98765 43210.
